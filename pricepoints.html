Price Points

When we research for different recipes that taste good, we also make sure that we keep them under a budget. To have a consistent result we use common grocery stores like ShopRite and Walmart. We decided to have a different cuisine per price range. Please note that all prices are per serving. Please visit the recipes page to view the recipes.

Our Indian dishes contain a variety of spices. These dishes range in the price range $5-$7

Our Thai dishes provide a variety of sweet and savory notes. These dishes range in the price range $7-$9.

For those who like American classics, We offer the goto dishes for comfort. The price range for these dishes is $8-$10.
